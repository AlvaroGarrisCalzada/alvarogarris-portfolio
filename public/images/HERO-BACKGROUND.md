# Hero Background Image

## Instructions

Place your hero background image here with the name: **hero-background.jpg**

### Recommended Specifications:
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Size**: 1920x1080px (Full HD) or higher
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9
- **File Size**: Under 500KB (compress if needed)
- **Content**: Should work well with text overlay (avoid busy patterns in center)

### Suggested Images:
- Abstract financial charts or data visualization
- Modern office/cityscape (blurred or subtle)
- Geometric patterns
- Minimalist gradient or texture
- Your workspace or professional setting

### Transparency Effect:
The image will be displayed with 90% opacity overlay to ensure text remains readable. The darker the background image, the more visible it will be through the overlay.

### Current Setup:
- Light mode: 90% white overlay
- Dark mode: 90% dark overlay
- Image covers full hero section
- Positioned behind all content with z-index layering
