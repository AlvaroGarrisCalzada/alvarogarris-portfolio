## Overview

Participated in **Deutsche Bank's intensive Summer Training Programme** in Madrid, a highly competitive early-career development program designed to provide comprehensive exposure to investment banking, trading, and corporate finance operations. Deutsche Bank is one of Europe's leading investment banks, with a global presence in corporate banking, investment banking, asset management, and private wealth management.

The programme offered structured rotations across multiple divisions including **Investment Banking, Trading, Corporate Finance, and Private Banking**, providing a 360-degree view of how an integrated financial institution operates. Through a combination of classroom training, trading simulations, desk rotations, and a capstone M&A project, I developed foundational skills in financial analysis, valuation, markets understanding, and professional communication.

This experience solidified my interest in pursuing a career in financial advisory and M&A, while building practical skills in financial modeling, company valuation, and deal analysis that have been instrumental in subsequent professional roles. The programme's rigorous structure and competitive environment fostered rapid learning and professional development.

## Descripción

Deutsche Bank es uno de los bancos de inversión líderes de Europa, con presencia global en banca corporativa, banca de inversión, gestión de activos y gestión de patrimonio privado.

El programa ofreció rotaciones estructuradas en múltiples divisiones incluyendo **Banca de Inversión, Trading, Finanzas Corporativas y Banca Privada**, proporcionando una visión 360 grados de cómo opera una institución financiera integrada. Mediante una combinación de formación en aula, simulaciones de trading, rotaciones de desk y un proyecto capstone de M&A, desarrollé habilidades fundamentales en análisis financiero, valoración, comprensión de mercados y comunicación profesional.

Esta experiencia solidificó mi interés en perseguir una carrera en asesoramiento financiero y M&A, mientras construía habilidades prácticas en modelización financiera, valoración de empresas y análisis de operaciones que han sido instrumentales en roles profesionales subsecuentes. La estructura rigurosa del programa y ambiente competitivo fomentaron aprendizaje rápido y desarrollo profesional.

## Programme Structure

### Week 1-2: Financial Markets Fundamentals & Trading
- **Capital Markets Overview**: Comprehensive introduction to equity markets, fixed income, foreign exchange, commodities, and derivatives
- **Market Microstructure**: Understanding order types, bid-ask spreads, market making, price discovery, and liquidity dynamics
- **Trading Simulations**: Participated in live trading simulations using Bloomberg Terminal, managing simulated portfolios and executing trades
- **Technical Analysis**: Introduction to charting techniques, trend analysis, support/resistance levels, and momentum indicators
- **Market Data Analysis**: Using Bloomberg functions (WRLD, TOP, MOVERS, etc.) to analyze market trends and news flow

### Week 3-4: M&A & Corporate Finance Training
- **Mergers & Acquisitions**: Full M&A process from origination to closing, including target identification, valuation, due diligence, and integration
- **Valuation Methodologies**: In-depth training on DCF analysis, comparable companies, precedent transactions, and LBO modeling
- **Deal Structuring**: Understanding deal structures including cash vs stock, earnouts, contingent consideration, and financing arrangements
- **Regulatory Considerations**: Overview of competition law, takeover regulations, shareholder approvals, and disclosure requirements
- **Case Studies**: Analyzed landmark M&A transactions including deal rationales, valuation methodologies, and outcome assessments

### Week 5-6: Product Group Rotations
- **Equity Capital Markets**: IPO process, secondary offerings, block trades, equity-linked instruments, valuation and pricing considerations
- **Debt Capital Markets**: Bond issuance process, credit ratings, pricing frameworks, yield curve analysis, corporate vs sovereign debt
- **Foreign Exchange & Rates**: Currency markets, interest rate products, hedging strategies, central bank interventions, carry trades
- **Structured Products**: Understanding complex financial instruments, risk-return profiles, and client suitability assessment
- **Desk Shadowing**: Spent time with traders and salespeople observing real-time market activity and client interactions

### Week 7-8: Final Project & Presentations
- **M&A Advisory Pitch Development**: Teams worked on comprehensive M&A pitch book for real or hypothetical transaction scenario
- **Financial Modeling**: Built detailed three-statement financial models, DCF valuations, and merger models with accretion/dilution analysis
- **Strategic Analysis**: Evaluated strategic fit, synergy potential, integration challenges, and value creation opportunities
- **Presentation Preparation**: Developed executive-level presentations synthesizing analysis into clear investment recommendation
- **Final Presentations**: Presented findings to senior Deutsche Bank leadership including Managing Directors and Division Heads

## Training Modules

### Financial Analysis & Accounting
- **Financial Statement Analysis**: In-depth review of income statements, balance sheets, cash flow statements, and notes to financials
- **Accounting Principles**: IFRS and US GAAP standards, revenue recognition, depreciation methods, working capital accounting
- **Financial Ratios**: Profitability margins, leverage ratios, liquidity metrics, efficiency ratios, and return metrics (ROE, ROIC, ROA)
- **Quality of Earnings**: Adjusting reported earnings for one-time items, non-cash charges, and accounting policy effects
- **Cash Flow Analysis**: Operating vs free cash flow, conversion rates, cash generation capabilities, and sustainability assessment

### Valuation Methodologies
- **Discounted Cash Flow (DCF)**: Unlevered DCF modeling, WACC calculation, terminal value estimation, sensitivity analysis
- **Trading Comparables**: Selecting peer groups, calculating and applying valuation multiples (EV/EBITDA, P/E, EV/Sales)
- **Transaction Comparables**: Identifying relevant precedent M&A transactions, control premiums, transaction multiples analysis
- **Leveraged Buyout (LBO)**: LBO modeling mechanics, sponsor returns calculation, exit multiple sensitivity, financing structure optimization
- **Sum-of-the-Parts**: Business segment valuation, conglomerate discount analysis, holding company structures

### Capital Markets
- **Equity Markets**: IPO process, follow-on offerings, rights issues, block trades, equity research, analyst coverage considerations
- **Debt Markets**: Investment grade vs high yield, bond pricing, credit spreads, ratings migration, refinancing considerations
- **Syndication Process**: Book building, price discovery, allocation strategies, stabilization activities, greenshoe options
- **Market Conditions**: Assessing market windows, investor appetite, comparable transaction timing, seasonal patterns
- **Regulatory Framework**: Prospectus requirements, roadshow regulations, lock-up periods, disclosure obligations

### Trading & Markets
- **Trading Strategies**: Market making, arbitrage opportunities, momentum trading, mean reversion, pairs trading concepts
- **Risk Management**: VaR calculations, stress testing, position limits, hedging strategies, counterparty risk assessment
- **Market Microstructure**: Order flow analysis, liquidity provision, bid-ask dynamics, high-frequency trading impacts
- **Derivatives**: Options strategies, futures contracts, swaps, structured products, risk-return profiles
- **Bloomberg Mastery**: Extensive training on Bloomberg Terminal functions for markets analysis, data retrieval, and news monitoring

### M&A Process & Execution
- **Deal Origination**: Target identification, screening criteria, preliminary valuation, strategic rationale assessment
- **Letter of Intent**: Negotiating key terms, exclusivity provisions, break fees, timing considerations
- **Due Diligence**: Financial, legal, tax, commercial, operational, IT, and environmental due diligence workstreams
- **Transaction Documentation**: Purchase agreements, disclosure schedules, representations and warranties, indemnification
- **Post-Merger Integration**: Synergy realization, organizational integration, systems integration, cultural alignment

## Final Project: M&A Advisory Pitch

### Project Scope
Led a **team of 4 participants** to complete a comprehensive M&A advisory pitch simulating a real-world transaction mandate. The project required applying all skills learned during the training programme to deliver an investment banking-quality presentation to senior Deutsche Bank leadership.

### Team Leadership & Collaboration
- **Project Management**: Organized team workstreams, established timelines, assigned responsibilities, and coordinated deliverables
- **Team Dynamics**: Fostered collaborative environment, resolved conflicts, leveraged individual strengths, and maintained motivation under pressure
- **Quality Control**: Reviewed all analysis and materials for accuracy, consistency, and professional presentation standards
- **Time Management**: Balanced multiple workstreams simultaneously while meeting tight deadlines typical of investment banking environment

### Target Company Analysis
- **Industry Research**: Conducted comprehensive industry analysis including market size, growth trends, competitive dynamics, and regulatory environment
- **Company Screening**: Evaluated 20+ potential target companies using quantitative screens (financials, valuation multiples) and qualitative criteria (strategic fit, management quality)
- **Target Selection**: Identified optimal acquisition target based on strategic rationale, financial profile, and value creation potential
- **Business Model Analysis**: Deep-dive into target's revenue streams, cost structure, competitive positioning, and growth drivers

### Financial Analysis & Valuation
- **Historical Analysis**: Built 5-year historical financial analysis including income statement, balance sheet, and cash flow statement
- **Financial Projections**: Developed detailed 5-year projections incorporating revenue growth assumptions, margin improvements, and working capital dynamics
- **DCF Valuation**: Performed unlevered DCF analysis with WACC calculation, terminal value estimation, and sensitivity analysis on key assumptions
- **Comparable Company Analysis**: Selected peer group of 8-10 public companies and applied trading multiples to derive valuation range
- **Precedent Transactions**: Analyzed 10+ recent M&A transactions in same sector to benchmark valuation multiples and control premiums
- **Valuation Summary**: Synthesized multiple valuation methodologies into recommended valuation range and implied offer price

### Synergy Analysis & Deal Rationale
- **Revenue Synergies**: Identified cross-selling opportunities, geographic expansion potential, product complementarity, and market share gains
- **Cost Synergies**: Quantified operational efficiencies, duplicate function elimination, procurement savings, and technology consolidation benefits
- **Synergy Quantification**: Estimated €25M+ in annual run-rate synergies with 3-year realization timeline
- **Strategic Benefits**: Articulated qualitative benefits including enhanced market position, diversification, and competitive advantages
- **Integration Roadmap**: Outlined high-level integration plan covering organization, systems, processes, and cultural alignment

### Financing Structure & Returns Analysis
- **Financing Mix**: Proposed optimal capital structure combining cash, debt, and equity consideration
- **Debt Capacity**: Analyzed pro forma leverage ratios, interest coverage, and compliance with banking covenants
- **Accretion/Dilution**: Performed merger model showing EPS accretion of 8% in year 1, increasing to 15% by year 3
- **IRR Analysis**: Calculated internal rate of return for private equity sponsor scenarios under various exit timing and multiple assumptions
- **Sensitivity Analysis**: Assessed impact of purchase price, synergy realization, and exit multiple variations on returns

### Presentation & Results
- **Pitch Book Development**: Produced professional 40-page pitch book with executive summary, company analysis, valuation, and recommendations
- **Presentation Delivery**: Presented findings to panel of 5 senior Deutsche Bank Managing Directors and Division Heads
- **Q&A Handling**: Fielded challenging questions on valuation assumptions, deal risks, integration challenges, and market conditions
- **Performance Recognition**: Achieved **top 3 ranking among 50+ programme participants**, receiving commendation for analytical rigor and presentation quality
- **Feedback**: Received praise for thorough analysis, clear communication, creative deal structuring, and teamwork
