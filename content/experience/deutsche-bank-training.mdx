## Overview

Completed Deutsche Bank's intensive summer training programme, gaining exposure to investment banking operations, trading, and corporate finance activities across multiple divisions.

## Programme Structure

- **Week 1-2**: Financial markets fundamentals and trading simulations
- **Week 3-4**: M&A and corporate finance training
- **Week 5-6**: Product group rotations (Equity, Fixed Income, FX)
- **Week 7-8**: Final project and presentations

## Training Modules

- Financial statement analysis and accounting principles
- Valuation methodologies (DCF, Trading Comps, Transaction Comps)
- Debt and equity capital markets overview
- Trading strategies and market microstructure
- Risk management and regulatory framework

## Final Project

Led a team of 4 to complete a full M&A advisory pitch, including:
- Target company identification and screening
- Comprehensive financial analysis and valuation
- Synergy analysis and deal rationale
- Financing structure recommendations

Presented findings to senior management and received top 3 ranking among 50+ participants.

## Key Takeaways

- Comprehensive understanding of investment banking product suite
- Practical experience in financial modeling under time pressure
- Exposure to German and European financial markets
- Professional development in teamwork and presentation skills
