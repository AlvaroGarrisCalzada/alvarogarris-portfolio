## Overview

As a **Strategy Consultant at Accenture Strategy**, I work at the intersection of business strategy and digital transformation, helping Fortune 500 companies and large enterprises navigate complex strategic challenges and unlock growth opportunities through technology-enabled solutions.

Accenture Strategy is the strategic consulting arm of Accenture, partnering with C-suite executives to drive sustainable growth, competitive advantage, and organizational transformation. My role involves conducting comprehensive strategic analyses, developing actionable recommendations, and supporting implementation of transformational initiatives across diverse industries including financial services, telecommunications, energy, and consumer goods.

Working in Madrid, I contribute to engagements that shape corporate strategies, digital roadmaps, and operating models for leading organizations. This position combines rigorous strategic frameworks with pragmatic implementation focus, leveraging Accenture's global capabilities and deep industry expertise to deliver measurable business impact.

## Descripción

Como **Consultor de Estrategia en Accenture Strategy**, trabajo en la intersección de estrategia empresarial y transformación digital, ayudando a empresas Fortune 500 y grandes corporaciones a navegar desafíos estratégicos complejos y desbloquear oportunidades de crecimiento mediante soluciones habilitadas por tecnología.
