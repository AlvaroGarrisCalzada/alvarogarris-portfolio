## Overview

As an **Economic Research Analyst at Arcano Partners**, I contributed to the firm's macro research capabilities, providing rigorous economic analysis and market intelligence to support institutional investment decisions and client advisory services. Arcano Partners is a leading independent Spanish financial advisory and asset management firm with expertise in fixed income, credit, and macroeconomic research.

Working within the research team, I conducted comprehensive **macroeconomic analysis** covering developed and emerging markets, with particular focus on European Central Bank monetary policy, sovereign debt dynamics, US fiscal policy, and global inflation trends. My work directly supported the firm's investment committee decision-making and client-facing research publications.

This role provided deep exposure to sell-side research methodologies, econometric modeling, and the intersection of macroeconomic analysis with fixed income markets. I developed expertise in interpreting central bank communications, analyzing economic data releases, and translating complex macroeconomic concepts into actionable investment insights for institutional clients.
