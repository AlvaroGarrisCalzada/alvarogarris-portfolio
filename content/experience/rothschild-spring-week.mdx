## Overview

Participated in Rothschild & Co's **highly competitive Spring Week programme** in London, one of the most prestigious early-career opportunities in investment banking. Rothschild & Co is a leading global independent financial advisory group, renowned for its M&A advisory excellence and trusted relationships with governments, corporations, and institutional investors worldwide.

During this intensive one-week programme, I gained comprehensive exposure to **M&A advisory operations**, investment banking workflows, and the strategic thinking that underpins major corporate transactions. The experience provided invaluable insights into deal origination, execution, and the client relationship management that defines Rothschild's position as a premier independent advisor.

Selected from thousands of applicants, the programme included live deal shadowing, technical training workshops, sector deep-dives, and extensive networking with senior bankers. This immersive experience solidified my understanding of the investment banking industry and affirmed my interest in pursuing a career in financial advisory and M&A.

## Descripción

Participé en el **programa Spring Week altamente competitivo de Rothschild & Co** en Londres, una de las oportunidades early-career más prestigiosas en banca de inversión. Rothschild & Co es un grupo de asesoramiento financiero independiente global líder, reconocido por su excelencia en asesoramiento M&A y relaciones de confianza con gobiernos, corporaciones e inversores institucionales mundialmente.

Durante este programa intensivo de una semana, obtuve exposición comprehensiva a **operaciones de asesoramiento M&A**, flujos de trabajo de banca de inversión y el pensamiento estratégico que sustenta transacciones corporativas mayores. La experiencia proporcionó insights invaluables sobre originación de operaciones, ejecución y gestión de relaciones con clientes que define la posición de Rothschild como asesor independiente premier.

Seleccionado entre miles de aplicantes, el programa incluyó seguimiento de operaciones en vivo, talleres de formación técnica, inmersiones sectoriales profundas y networking extensivo con banqueros senior. Esta experiencia inmersiva solidificó mi comprensión de la industria de banca de inversión y afirmó mi interés en perseguir una carrera en asesoramiento financiero y M&A.

## Programme Highlights

### Live M&A Deal Exposure
- **Shadowed senior bankers** working on active M&A transactions across multiple sectors, gaining firsthand insight into deal execution
- Observed pitch preparation, client meetings, due diligence coordination, and deal structuring discussions
- Learned about the full M&A lifecycle from origination and mandate winning through execution and deal closure
- Understood the role of independent advisors in providing conflict-free advice to clients on strategic transactions

### Financial Modeling & Valuation Training
- Participated in **intensive technical training workshops** covering advanced financial modeling, valuation methodologies, and deal analysis
- Built DCF models, performed comparable company analysis, and evaluated precedent transactions
- Learned industry-specific valuation considerations across different sectors and transaction types
- Received training on using Bloomberg Terminal, Capital IQ, and Excel for financial analysis

### Sector Deep-Dives
- Engaged in comprehensive **sector workshops** covering Technology, Media & Telecommunications (TMT), Healthcare, Industrials, and Financial Services
- Analyzed sector-specific trends, key value drivers, regulatory considerations, and competitive dynamics
- Reviewed recent notable transactions and discussed strategic rationales behind major deals
- Understood how sector expertise informs advisory work and client relationship development

### Networking & Professional Development
- Extensive interaction with **Managing Directors, Directors, Vice Presidents, and Analysts** across Rothschild's London office
- Participated in panel discussions about career progression in investment banking and financial advisory
- Gained insights into Rothschild's culture, values, and approach to client service
- Developed professional network within one of the world's most respected independent advisory firms

### Group Project & Presentation
- Collaborated with fellow participants on a **live case study** involving strategic analysis and M&A advisory recommendation
- Conducted market research, competitive analysis, and valuation work to support strategic recommendations
- Prepared and delivered presentation to senior bankers, receiving constructive feedback on analytical approach and communication style
- Developed teamwork and time management skills under tight deadlines
