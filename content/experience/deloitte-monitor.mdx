## Overview

As a Financial Advisory Analyst at **Deloitte's Monitor Division** in Barcelona and Zaragoza, I worked on high-stakes M&A transactions, corporate valuations, and strategic advisory engagements for mid-market and large-cap clients across Spain and Europe. My role combined rigorous financial analysis with strategic thinking to support corporate decision-making in complex deal environments.

Deloitte Monitor is Deloitte's premier strategy and corporate finance practice, serving as trusted advisors to C-suite executives and private equity firms on transformative transactions. During my tenure, I contributed to multiple deal teams, conducting due diligence, building sophisticated financial models, and delivering insights that shaped investment decisions worth hundreds of millions of euros.

This experience provided me with deep exposure to the entire M&A lifecycle—from target screening and valuation to due diligence execution and post-merger integration planning. I developed expertise in financial modeling, industry analysis, and client communication while working alongside senior partners and directors on time-sensitive, high-pressure engagements.
