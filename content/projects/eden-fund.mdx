## Fund Overview

**EDEN Fund** is the first student-led investment fund in Spain, created to replicate a professional buy-side environment and provide hands-on experience in global macro investing, portfolio management, and quantitative research.

Founded at **IE University in 2021**, EDEN operates a diversified, multi-asset portfolio inspired by an **All-Weather framework**, allocating capital across equities, macro-thematic strategies, fixed income, and real assets.

## Descripción del Fondo

**EDEN Fund** es el primer fondo de inversión dirigido por estudiantes en España, creado para replicar un entorno profesional buy-side y proporcionar experiencia práctica en inversión macro global, gestión de carteras e investigación cuantitativa.

Fundado en **IE University en 2021**, EDEN opera una cartera diversificada multi-activo inspirada en un **marco All-Weather**, asignando capital en acciones, estrategias macro-temáticas, renta fija y activos reales.

### Investment Philosophy
- **All-Weather Framework**: Designed to perform across economic cycles
- **Risk Management Focus**: Strong emphasis on downside protection
- **Disciplined Allocation**: Multi-asset diversification across economic regimes
- **Quantitative Approach**: Data-driven decision making with backtesting and scenario analysis

### Performance Highlights
- Navigated highly volatile market conditions (2021-2024)
- Achieved positive absolute returns
- Superior risk-adjusted performance vs S&P 500
- Demonstrated resilience during market drawdowns
